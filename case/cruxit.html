CASE STUDY

How Crux IT Consulting achieved a 65% close rate on qualified leads

IT Services & eLearning Consultancy UK & India LMS, Salesforce & App Development

The challenge

Crux IT Consulting Services is a UK and India-based IT consultancy delivering eLearning solutions, LMS development, Salesforce implementation, mobile application development, and embedded systems. They serve corporate, education, government, and healthcare clients with end-to-end IT services.

Crux had been through a series of poor experiences with marketing agencies. Each had promised leads but delivered contacts that didn't match their services or the scale of projects they deliver. The team was spending too much time qualifying unsuitable prospects, and the pipeline remained inconsistent.

The approach

Crux's niche is specific: organisations that need custom eLearning, LMS administration, Salesforce consulting, or application development. During our discovery call, we worked closely with the team to define exactly what a qualified lead looks like: companies with genuine project budgets, clear requirements, and a timeline.

We built campaigns that spoke directly to these buyers, positioning Crux's breadth of services as a competitive advantage. Rather than generic IT consulting messaging, we highlighted their specific capabilities in eLearning, LMS, and Salesforce to attract the right decision-makers.

The commission-only model was particularly appealing to Crux after their previous negative agency experiences. Zero risk meant they could test the partnership without another costly commitment.

Over 6 months, we delivered 117 qualified leads. The close rate of 65% was exceptional, driven by the precision of our targeting. With an average project value of £72,000, every lead that converted represented significant revenue. The results completely changed Crux's perception of agency partnerships.
