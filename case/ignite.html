CASE STUDY

How Ignite Consulting generated 97 qualified leads in 4 months

Innovation & Change Consultancy Public Sector Focus Employee-Owned (EOT)

The challenge

Ignite Consulting is an innovation and change consultancy founded in 2004, specialising in transforming public services. With a team of experienced consultants, they deliver digital transformation, organisational design, and change management for councils, government bodies, and private sector organisations.

Despite a strong reputation and two decades of experience, Ignite relied primarily on referrals for new business. They had tried several agencies before, but none delivered leads that matched the quality and complexity of the projects Ignite takes on. The pipeline was unpredictable, and growth was limited to whoever happened to refer them next.

The approach

We started with a 30-minute discovery call to understand Ignite's ideal client profile: mid-to-large public sector organisations undergoing transformation, with budgets that matched the scale of work Ignite delivers.

We built targeted campaigns across Google and Meta, designed to reach decision-makers at councils and public bodies actively seeking consulting support for digital transformation and organisational change. Every lead was pre-qualified against the criteria we defined together.

Ignite paid nothing upfront. Our commission model meant we only earned when their leads converted into signed projects.

Within the first month, Ignite had qualified conversations with exactly the type of organisations they wanted to work with. Over 4 months, we delivered 97 qualified leads. With a 40% close rate and an average project value of £35,000, the return on investment was substantial.
