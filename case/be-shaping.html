CASE STUDY

How Be Shaping the Future generated 210 qualified leads in 10 months

Management & Technology Consultancy Financial Services Focus Part of Engineering Group

The challenge

Be | Shaping the Future UK is the UK subsidiary of a pan-European management and technology consultancy, specialising in the financial services industry. They deliver programme management, data analytics, operating model design, regulatory compliance, and technology strategy for banks, capital markets firms, and payments companies.

Despite being part of a larger group with over 1,700 employees globally, the UK arm needed its own pipeline of qualified leads. They had tried multiple agencies and even had another agency on retainer when they engaged Elevate. The existing agency was delivering volume, but not the quality of leads that matched Be UK's niche in financial services consulting.

The approach

During our discovery call, we mapped Be UK's ideal client profile: financial services organisations needing consulting support for regulatory change, digital transformation, and operational resilience. The target was senior decision-makers at UK banks, asset managers, and payments firms.

We built campaigns specifically targeting this niche, using precise messaging that resonated with financial services buyers. Rather than broad "IT consulting" positioning, we focused on the specific pain points Be UK solves: regulatory compliance, risk management, and operating model transformation.

Be UK maintained their existing agency relationship initially, which gave us the opportunity to prove the difference in lead quality side by side.

The results

£42k

Over 10 months, we delivered 210 qualified leads from the financial services sector. With a 37% close rate and an average project value of £42,000, the results spoke for themselves. The difference in lead quality was so clear that Be UK ended their contract with the other agency within weeks.
